About Us

The Robo-Colts are a competitive second-year robotics team from Southwest Sugar Land, bringing STEM opportunities to our community. As World Championship qualifiers in our rookie year, we demonstrate excellence in robotics, innovation, and teamwork.


We're the first prominent FRC team in Southwest Houston, providing unique opportunities for students to develop STEM skills and leadership abilities.
